Winter Fair 2025 Raffle

Jubilee Primary School PTA

Thank you so much for supporting the Jubilee PTA Winter Fair Raffle. Every ticket helps raise funds for a new piece of equipment for the KS2 playground, which will be enjoyed daily by children across the school. All proceeds (minus small payment processing fees) go directly to Jubilee PTA to support this project.

How the Raffle Works

This year's raffle is 100% online*. When you buy tickets via the payment link below, we collect your name, email and phone number. Your email receipt is your ticket, so check it has the correct number of tickets listed. Instead of using ticket numbers this year, we'll announce winners by name at the draw on 5 December.
